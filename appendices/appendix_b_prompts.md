# Appendix B: AI-Assisted Writing Prompts

In this study, participants interacted with the AI model using a standardized prompt structure to ensure consistency across the experimental group.

### System Prompt (AI Persona)
"You are a professional academic writing assistant. Your goal is to provide constructive feedback on L2 academic essays. Focus on coherence, vocabulary diversity, and sentence structure. Do not rewrite the essay for the student; explain your suggestions clearly."

### User Prompt Structure
"Review the following paragraph from my academic essay. Provide feedback on:
1. Vocabulary choices (specifically synonyms for academic tone).
2. Sentence complexity.
3. Logical flow.
[Insert Writer Paragraph Here]"
